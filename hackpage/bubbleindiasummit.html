Introduction

Bringing together the vibrant Indian Bubble community who are interested in building powerful software without code.
Big Bubble Hackathon:

  1. Think about the problem. Get inspirations or build an idea of your own.
  2. Build it using Bubble. Or any other low/no-code tool you're comfortable with. But some part of it must involve Bubble.
  3. Launch and deploy it. Run this by other attendees, or #buildinpublic.
